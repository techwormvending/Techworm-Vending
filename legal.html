Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2025 Current

Techworm Vending LLC ("Techworm Vending," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers, venue partners, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information – name, email address, phone number, and company name submitted through our contact forms or sales inquiries.
  • Transaction data – payment method type (card or tap-to-pay) and purchase records processed through our vending machines. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Machine interaction data – items selected, purchase timestamps, and machine location identifiers for inventory and operational purposes.
  • SMS opt-in data – if you opt into SMS communications, we record your consent, phone number, and opt-in date.
  • Website usage data – IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs collected through standard web analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfill purchases and process payments at our vending machines.
  • To respond to inquiries submitted through our contact form or by phone.
  • To send SMS messages you have opted into, including service updates, sales follow-ups, and support communications.
  • To restock and maintain our machines based on sales data.
  • To improve our product offerings and predict customer demand.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. SMS Consent

SMS consent and phone numbers collected for SMS communications are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers – payment processors, SMS platforms, and analytics providers who assist us in operating our business, bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Venue partners – only aggregate, non-identifiable sales data is shared with location partners.
  • Legal authorities – when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of Techworm Vending or others.

5. Camera & AI Vision Technology

Our vending machines use AI vision technology to detect items removed from the machine. Machine cameras capture only product-level interactions — items and hands within the machine interior. Cameras do not capture faces, surrounding spaces, or identifiable individuals. No facial recognition is used. All machines are compliant with applicable Pennsylvania state laws.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Transaction and sales data may be retained for up to 7 years for financial recordkeeping purposes.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data where applicable.
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Payment data is handled through PCI-compliant processors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be posted at techwormvending.com with a revised effective date.
