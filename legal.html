Privacy Policy & Terms

Techworm Vending LLC · Last updated: July 1, 2026

Who We Are

Techworm Vending LLC ("Techworm," "we," "us") operates AI-powered smart coolers in Pennsylvania. Our machines are refrigerated coolers with glass doors: you tap or insert a card, open the door, take what you want, and the machine's AI vision system charges you automatically for the items you removed. An optional QR code appears on the screen after each purchase so you can view a digital receipt.

This page explains what information is collected when you use our machines or our website, how it's used, and your choices. Questions? Email support@techwormvending.com or call (412) 779-9809.

Cameras & AI Vision

Our cameras never film you. All cameras are fully contained inside each cooler and capture only the products on the shelves and hands reaching for items. They do not capture faces, bodies, or the surrounding space, and we do not use facial recognition of any kind.

The interior vision system exists for one purpose: to detect which products were removed from the cooler so your card can be charged accurately. Image data is processed to identify products and resolve billing disputes, and is not used to identify individuals.

Our machines are operated in compliance with Pennsylvania law. No external cameras are installed on or around our machines unless specifically requested by the venue.

Payment Information

When you tap or insert your card at a machine, the transaction is processed by our third-party payment processor. Techworm does not see, store, or have access to your full card number. We receive only the transaction details needed to run the business: items purchased, amount charged, date, time, and machine location, along with a tokenized reference used for refunds and dispute resolution.

Payment processing is handled by PCI-compliant providers. Their handling of your card data is governed by their own privacy policies.

Pre-authorization holds

A temporary hold may appear on your card when you open the door. The hold is adjusted to the actual purchase amount after the door closes, or released if nothing is taken. Release timing depends on your bank, typically 1–5 business days.

Receipts

Scanning the QR code on the screen after a purchase is optional. If you scan it, your device opens a receipt page for that transaction. We do not collect your contact information through the receipt unless you choose to provide it.

Website & Contact Form

When you submit our contact form, the information you provide (such as your name, email address, phone number, company, and message) is transmitted to us via Formspree, a third-party form service, and delivered to our inbox. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and, where relevant, to follow up about placing a machine at your location.

We do not sell your contact information. We do not add you to marketing lists without your consent.

How We Use Data

We use purchase and inventory data to:

  • Charge you correctly for the items you take
  • Process refunds and resolve billing disputes
  • Track inventory and generate restocking schedules so machines stay full and food stays fresh
  • Analyze sales trends to decide which products to stock at each location and anticipate what customers will want
  • Maintain, monitor, and improve machine performance

All sales analysis is done at the product and machine level, not the individual level. We are interested in what sells, not who bought it.

Data Sharing

We share data only with service providers who help us operate: payment processors, machine telemetry and inventory platforms, and form/email services. We may share aggregate, non-identifying sales summaries (for example, total monthly sales) with the venue hosting a machine. We may disclose information if required by law. We never sell personal information.

SMS consent: Phone numbers and SMS opt-in consent collected for text messaging are not shared with, or sold to, any third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for assistance at any time.

SMS / Text Messaging

Techworm Vending uses SMS for one-on-one conversational messaging only: responding to inquiries, coordinating machine service visits, and following up with venue contacts and customers who reach out to us. We do not send bulk marketing texts.

How you opt in

You opt in to receive text messages from us by checking the optional SMS consent box on our website contact form, or by verbally requesting text communication, in which case our first message confirms your opt-in and includes opt-out instructions. Consent to receive text messages is never required to contact us, purchase from our machines, or do business with us.

How you opt out

Reply STOP to any message to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance, or contact support@techwormvending.com or (412) 779-9809. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

Data Retention & Security

Transaction records are retained as required for accounting, tax, and dispute-resolution purposes. Interior camera data is retained only as long as needed for billing accuracy and dispute resolution, then deleted. We use reputable, secured third-party platforms and restrict access to business data to authorized personnel.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Refunds & disputes: Contact support@techwormvending.com with the machine location, approximate time, and last four digits of your card. We respond promptly.
  • Contact form data: You may request that we delete your inquiry information at any time.
  • Questions: You may ask us what categories of data we hold in connection with a transaction.
